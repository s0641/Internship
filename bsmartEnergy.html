bsmartEnergy

bSMART-Energy is a web based independent application for metering, billing, collection and Customer Information Services. The robust billing engine is highly configurable for various billing process like Spot Billing, online billing, batch billing, bulk billing etc. The UI based tariff management gives the required flexibility to configure and manage the tariff changes. The built in dashboards and customer profiling give a real-time picture of various key parameters that require constant monitoring by the utility. Drill down built in reports cover all the statutory reporting requirements of the utility, further the custom report generation tool facilitates any ad-hoc report requirements. Energy audit feature gives a real-time energy utilization picture.
