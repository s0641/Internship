Enumeration of Ipsets and DTCs using BSmartGIS a OGC (Open Geospatial Consortium) at CESC (A Government of Karnataka Undertaking)

THE SCOPE

1. The Enumeration and GIS mapping of IP sets as per Enumeration and indicate them on a web based OGC (Open Geospatial Consortium) compliant GIS software application. The details of talukas coming under Depleted water level area has to be collected.

2. The details of IP sets by the jurisdictional Executive Engineer/Engineer, O &M Division, CESC and create a database.

3. Once the actual data is collected from the field the same has to be digitized, mapped and loaded onto a web based OGC compliant of GIS software application.

4. The Enumeration of IP sets have to be carried out on a regular basis and to be maintained in the GIS software for stipulated time.

5. To incorporate facility to mash up the location information of IP sets and on standard base map such as Google.

6. To make provision to view entire GIS data of IP sets provided to all, but edit and update option should be given to the concerned Assistant Executive Engineer(Elec.,) of the respective sub division by creating user access credentials. The last updated date and time should be recorded in the system.

7. Assessment of connected load of the IP sets in terms of HP to be arrived using tong tester reading of Current & Voltage with power factor as 0.85.

8. GIS should depict IP sets as different points with different color codes for easy identification. Each point to have its associated data as attributes and this data to be visible when the point is clicked upon.

THE CHALLENGE

1.The survey of large sets of IPsets in stipulated time 2. Maintenance of collected ipsets and dtc on the database 3. Plotting of the collected databases on BSmartGIS ( collection of Open Geospatial Consortium ). 4. Login based on the Circle, Divison, Sub division and section level and depicting the related hierarchy ipsets and DTCs . 5. Showing the number of Ipsets connected with that of dtc by line diagram 6. Area Hierarchy e. Circle, Divison, Sub division and section level ipsets and DTCs 7. All functionalities in the software accessible through mobile phones and tablet, PCs etc on internet facility. 8. The application have the required security for login authentication, security against hacking, etc.

THE SOLUTION

1. Accurate and quality Enumeration of Ipsets and DTCs by the dedicated team.

2 .Dedicated team for the Database management.

3. The dedicated team for BSmartGIS for the connecting the database on OGC.

4. BsmartGIS shows the details of IP sets and DTCs by the jurisdictional Executive Engineer/Engineer, O &M Division, CESC and create a database.

5. The Ipsets and DTCs consists of attributes related to the location, RRNo, Date of service, Load measured in HP, from which DTCs the IP set fed, depleted water level area and type of water sources (Open well, Borewell, Nala and river).

THE HIGHLIGHTS

1. A Fully web based OGC compliant GIS software was developed and deployed .

2 .More then 80,000 DTCs and 3,00,000 IP Sets enumerated.

3. Area of enumeration is spanning around 27858 Sqkm.

4. Total 5 districts are covered.

5. The entire software developed using Open source technologies

6. Quality Checking of data was done with advance web based tools.

7. Advance Mobile based technologies were used for enumeration.
